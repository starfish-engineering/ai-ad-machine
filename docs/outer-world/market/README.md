## Market Overview

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)
- Global digital advertising market: $600B+ annually
- PPC management tools market: ~$5B and growing 15% YoY

### Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)
- SMB and mid-market PPC advertisers: ~$2B
- Focus: English-speaking markets (US, UK, AU, CA)

### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)
- Year 1 target: $5M ARR
- Year 3 target: $25M ARR

## Industry Trends

### 1. AI Transformation
- Platform AI (Google PMax, Meta ASC) is changing the game
- Need for AI that complements, not competes with platform AI
- "AI that empowers marketers, not replaces them"

### 2. Privacy & Measurement Evolution
- Cookie deprecation changing attribution
- First-party data becoming critical
- Server-side tracking adoption

### 3. Platform Consolidation
- Cross-platform management becoming essential
- Unified reporting across channels
- Budget optimization across platforms

### 4. Automation Acceleration
- Manual optimization becoming untenable
- Rule-based automation as baseline
- AI-driven predictive optimization emerging

### 5. Agency Efficiency Pressure
- Margin pressure on agencies
- Need for scalable operations
- White-label solutions in demand

## Customer Personas

### 1. Agency Account Manager - "Sarah"

**Profile:**
- 28-35 years old
- Manages 15-30 client accounts
- 5-10 years PPC experience
- Uses multiple platforms daily

**Pain Points:**
- Context switching between accounts
- Repetitive optimization tasks
- Client reporting takes too long
- Missing issues until clients complain

**Goals:**
- Manage more accounts efficiently
- Never miss a critical issue
- Impress clients with insights
- Grow into leadership role

**Quote:** "I need to know about problems before my clients do."

---

### 2. In-House Marketing Manager - "Mike"

**Profile:**
- 30-40 years old
- Single brand, multiple campaigns
- Reports to CMO/VP Marketing
- Budget: $50K-500K/month

**Pain Points:**
- Proving ROI to leadership
- Balancing brand vs. performance
- Budget pacing and forecasting
- Staying current with platform changes

**Goals:**
- Hit revenue/ROAS targets
- Efficient budget utilization
- Clear attribution reporting
- Competitive advantage

**Quote:** "I need to justify every dollar to my CFO."

---

### 3. Freelance PPC Specialist - "Alex"

**Profile:**
- 25-40 years old
- Manages 5-15 client accounts
- Self-employed or small agency
- Price-sensitive

**Pain Points:**
- Limited time for each account
- Can't afford enterprise tools
- Need to appear professional
- Administrative overhead

**Goals:**
- Take on more clients
- Automate routine work
- Professional reporting
- Sustainable business

**Quote:** "I'm one person wearing ten hats."

---

### 4. Enterprise Director of Performance - "Jennifer"

**Profile:**
- 35-50 years old
- Oversees team of 5-20
- Multi-million dollar budgets
- Complex stakeholder management

**Pain Points:**
- Security and compliance requirements
- Integration with martech stack
- Team coordination and access
- Global/regional complexity

**Goals:**
- Operational excellence
- Risk management
- Team productivity
- Strategic insights

**Quote:** "I need enterprise-grade everything."

## Market Entry Strategy

### Phase 1: Beachhead (Year 1)
- Focus: US-based agencies with 10-50 accounts
- Channel: Content marketing, PPC community engagement
- Goal: 500 paying customers, $500K ARR

### Phase 2: Expansion (Year 2)
- Focus: In-house teams, expand to UK/AU
- Channel: Add partnerships, paid acquisition
- Goal: 2,000 customers, $3M ARR

### Phase 3: Scale (Year 3+)
- Focus: Enterprise, global expansion
- Channel: Sales team, channel partners
- Goal: 5,000+ customers, $15M+ ARR
