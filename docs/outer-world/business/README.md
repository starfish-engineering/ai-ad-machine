## Value Proposition

**"Worry-free PPC management in the AI era."**

Draper delivers a unique combination of:
1. **Automation with Control** - Set it and trust it, but stay in command
2. **Protection & Guardrails** - Never wake up to a blown budget
3. **Actionable Intelligence** - Not just data, but recommendations
4. **Human Support** - Real people for onboarding and support

## Customer Segments

### Primary Segments

| Segment | Size | Characteristics | Key Needs |
|---------|------|-----------------|-----------|
| **PPC Agencies** | Large | Managing 10-500+ client accounts | Efficiency, white-label, reporting |
| **In-House Teams** | Medium | 1-20 campaigns, internal brands | Optimization, budget control |
| **Freelancers** | Large | Solo, managing 1-20 accounts | Time savings, templates |
| **Enterprise** | Small | Complex, multi-market, compliance | Security, integrations, scale |

### Segment-Specific Value Props

**Agencies:**
- White-label dashboards and reports
- Bulk operations across accounts
- Client management and permissions
- Agency-specific pricing tiers

**In-House Teams:**
- Deep integration with internal tools
- Advanced attribution modeling
- Budget pacing and forecasting
- Team collaboration features

**Freelancers:**
- Quick account audits
- Pre-built automation templates
- Time-tracking for billing
- Affordable solo plans

**Enterprise:**
- SSO and security compliance
- Custom integrations via API
- Dedicated account management
- SLA guarantees

   - Marketplace Ads Toolkit
